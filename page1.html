Amazon Trailers

AZNG Trailers have the Amazon Logo on the side.


They appear either Blue or White with the Amazon Smile


Drivers might bring in an AZNG trailer and pick up another AZNG or different type of trailer.

In this case, you'll have to gather the VRID for a Drop Off and another one for a Pick Up
