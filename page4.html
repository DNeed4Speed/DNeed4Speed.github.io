Non-Inventory

These are annoying. Mostly, these are loads brought in by Drivers who aren't aware they are Non-Inventory


The best scenario is the Driver actually tells you they have a Non-Inventory load


The only advice is:

if you are given a piece of paper - check for a 2D label on it


Otherwise, you will have to Radio a different Channel


The Channels you will have to radio are either:


Department Channel How Often
Non-Inventory 9 95%
Safety/Hazmat 1 5%

When in doubt, Radio TOM Team (A TOM Lead) for extra help
