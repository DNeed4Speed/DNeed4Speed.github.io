Check - In: Outbound

- Outbound Pickup

  • Verify the Tractor License Plate matches on GEM
  • Gather the last four digits of the VRID
  • Ask for the Driver's Phone Number
  • Enter the License Plate, VRID and Phone Number onto Slack - under tom-check-in-checkout

    • Here's how the info would look on Slack:

    YP01477

    VRID 115BJVPMM

    408 992 5066


  • Enter the VRID on SSP (Outbound) to see if the Outbound Pickup is ready. Primarily check if it says Finished Loading as well as any scheduled Hostler Moves

    • Note: 2 Locations the Outbound Trailer is located are either a Dock Door or Full Slip (301-313)


    Finished Loading: You can check with Ship Clerk that the load is actaully ready by messaging the tom-ship-clerk-escalations chat typing " *VRID*, is this load ready? @present member ". If it is, send the Driver to the Dock Door the Trailer is located

    Note: Make sure the Driver knows to attach to the Trailer on a Green Light

    Hostler Moves: if there is a scheduled move for the Outbound Trailer a Driver is picking up and a Driver is here to pick up the same Trailer, on the Radio say: Yard Drivers, who's in the move for (dock door to the full slips), I have the Driver here at the gate."


  • If a Driver's Outbound Load is not ready, inform the Driver "Your load isn't ready, please park along the fence and wait for us to call you." Send the Driver into the Yard
