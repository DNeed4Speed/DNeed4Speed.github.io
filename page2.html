Amazon UPS Intermodal Non-Inventory Pup
UPS Trailers

UPS Drivers often pick up an Outbound load


They often carry an EMPTY UPS Trailer/Rental or PARCEL LOADED trailer on arrival


The main UPS Outbound loads drivers will pick up are:

UPS-NEXT-WEST

UPS-2DAY

UPS-LACHA-T


Just like with AZNGs, be sure to verify the Trailer the UPS is carrying then the one they are picking up

The Trailer UPS Drivers are carrying DON'T come with a VRID


Here are some scenarios you'll encounter:

Arrival Reason Location
Bobtail Picking up Loaded Dock Door or Slip
Tractor with Empty Trailer Dropping Empty / Picking Up Loaded Empty: Slip / Loaded: Dock Door
Tractor with Loaded Trailer (PARCEL) Dropping Loaded / Picking Up Loaded Drop: Slip / Pick Up: Dock Door

Pro Tip #1: Tell UPS Drivers which Dock Door their Outbound load will be - they're good boys


Pro Tip #2: The bold UPS Driver with shades always wants paperwork
