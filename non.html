Check - In: Non-Inventory

- Non-Inventory Dropoff

  • This will require some deducing, sometimes Drivers are not aware they are hauling a Non-Inventory trailer
  • Confrim the Tractor License Plate and Trailer ID using the cameras and GEM
  • Once verified, click Unscheduled Appointment and select Non-Inventory Dropoff
  • Ideally, the Driver will tell you they have a trailer for Non-Inventory. If not, find out by asking to see their paperwork - if there is a 2D number or Non-Inventory listed then the trailer is for Non-Invnetory
  • Assign the Driver one of the Non-Inventory slips (these have NI next to them) and Check them In
  • For Fresh: send the trailer to Dock Doors 98-100. The Drivers who bring in these trailers for Fresh usually know where to go already, Check them In first.
