Check - In: Reactive

- Reactive Dropoff

  • When a REACTIVE appears at the Entry Gate, under Reason, Reactive will be listed
  • Verify the Tractor License Plate, Trailer ID and VRID
  • Radio or Slack Inbound

    • Radio: "Inbound, I have a Reactive (list the location it came from and the last 4 of the VRID) where do you want it?"

    Slack: Message Inbound in the tom-inbound chat that you are checking in a Reactive

    Note: Inbound will leave a picture in the tom-inbound Slack chat of expected Reactives and their designated Dock Doors. However, not all Reactives will be assigned a Dock Door and assigned Dock Doors will not always be available. Check with Inbound if not sure.

  • Once you know fully where to send the Reactive, in GEM update the Tractor Location to Tractor Only and assign the location Inbound listed for you. Post the following information on the tom-inbound Slack chat

    • Example:

    BF14->OAK4

    TansfersReactive

    ISA 628345607

    VRID 1132YTGSD

    126

    Note: Make sure you DON'T place a REACTIVE in the Yard. If you can't get a hold of Inbound or if there are no free Dock Doors, send the Reactive to the North Fence and notify Inbound

    Sending a REACTIVE to the Fence:

    @present member sending this Reactive to the Fence

    BF14->OAK4

    TansfersReactive

    ISA 628345607

    VRID 1132YTGSD

    North Fence


  • Unless the Driver has a wire-cutter or bolt-cutter, Radio a Hostler Driver to meet the Driver in the Yard and cut the seal
  • Radio the Hostler Drivers: "Yard Drivers, I have a Reactive going to (location), can one of you cut the seal?"
